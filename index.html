Harmonic Burn Curve
Medium
Every transfer burns an additional amount on top of the 2.5% tax, but the burn rate follows a harmonic decay: burn% = 0.5% × (current_supply / 10M). As supply drops, burns slow asymptotically toward zero, creating a natural supply floor around 2M tokens. Early transfers are aggressively deflationary; late-stage is stable.

High
A transfer hook dynamically routes the 2.5% tax to different destinations based on on-chain state. Treasury below threshold? 100% goes there. LP depth too low? Feeds liquidity incentives. Otherwise, buyback-and-burn. The tax becomes a self-balancing economic system rather than a flat drain.
